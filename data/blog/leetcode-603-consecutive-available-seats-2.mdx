## 정답

```
SELECT seat_id
FROM
    (SELECT
            CASE
                WHEN LEAD(free,1) OVER (ORDER BY seat_id) = 1 AND free = 1 THEN seat_id
                WHEN LAG(free,1) OVER (ORDER BY seat_id) = 1 AND free = 1 THEN seat_id
            END AS seat_id
    FROM Cinema) AS Temp
WHERE seat_id IS NOT NULL
```
