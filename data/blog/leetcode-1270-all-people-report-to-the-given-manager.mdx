## Solution

```
SELECT E1.employee_id
FROM Employees AS E1, Employees AS E2, Employees AS E3
WHERE 
    E1.manager_id = E2.employee_id AND
    E2.manager_id = E3.employee_id AND
    E3.manager_id = 1 AND e1.employee_id != 1
```
