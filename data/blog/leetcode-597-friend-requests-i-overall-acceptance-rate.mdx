## 정답

```
SELECT ROUND(IFNULL(
        (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM (SELECT DISTINCT requester_id, accepter_id FROM RequestAccepted) AS T1) /
        (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM (SELECT DISTINCT sender_id, send_to_id FROM FriendRequest) AS T2),
        0),2) AS accept_rate

```
